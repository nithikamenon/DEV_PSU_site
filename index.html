Who I Am

I would describe myself as creative, curious, and energized. Creating is my favorite thing to do. Most of my hobbies are creative outlets from drawing, to baking, to fashion. This website you're on is another example- the first website I've ever made!

My curiousity shines best through my love for learning. From the classes I take to watching documentaries or visiting museums, I love to keep my mind interested.

Part of who I am is shaped by my grandfather who is one of my role models. His humility and hardwork have always been something I have admired. The top photo shows my grandfather and I sharing a birthday celebration.

I love to engage in the world around me. It keeps me excited and allows me to create new experiences. The bottom photo shows a laughing Buddha snowglobe that I found at a collectables store. I found it delightful and amusing.
